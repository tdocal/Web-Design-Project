Rocky Mountain Conservancy

Park Restoration Projects

This map shows the locations of the conservation work done by The Rocky Mountain Conservancy across the Rocky Mountains.

Click on the pins to see a brief description of the work that has been completed. The images are placeholder, but will be updated to show before and after conditions at a later date. Use the home button to reset the view to the default setting. Use the button below the home button in the upper right to change the basemap.

Data provided by The Rocky Mountain Conservancy, February 2018

Reported Cases of Chlamydia in 2016

This map shows the number of cases of chlamydia reported in 2016, according to the CDC. Choose the census division from the list on the bottom left to see the breakdown of reported cases in each region of the country.

The height of each county represents the number of cases reported, while the color depth represents the percentage of each county's population that was diagnosed with chlamydia.

It is possible to load every region at the same time, however, it requires 16GB of RAM and I don't think it's a reasonable assumption that everyone has that much. So, I've broken the map down into pieces that can be selected one at a time. In the future I will add similar maps of other STDs as reported by the CDC as well as a timeline that shows the increase in reported cases over time. However, that is not something I will be able to accomplish by the end of the semester.

Data provided by the CDC and AtlasPlus, September 2018.

Denver Bridge Integrity

This map shows the structural integrity of each bridge in Denver as rated by the FHWA.

Bridges are determined to be Structurally Deficient if the Deck, Substructure, Superstructure, or Culvert receive a rating of 4 or below or if the bridge receives an appraisal rating of 2 or below.

This map hasn't been started yet and may change in the future, right now it links to one of the other maps already created.

Data provided by the FHWA and USGS, September 2018.
