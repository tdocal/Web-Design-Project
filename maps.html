    Rocky Mountain Conservancy

    Park Restoration Projects

    This map shows the locations of the conservation work done by The Rocky Mountain Conservancy across the Rocky Mountains.

    Click on the pins to see a brief description of the work completed. The images are placeholder, but will be updated to show before and after conditions at a later date. Use the home button to reset the view to the default setting. Use the button below the home button in the upper right to change the basemap.

    Data provided by The Rocky Mountain Conservancy, February 2018

    Reported Cases of Chlamydia in 2016

    In August of 2018 the CDC released a report stating the number of cases of STDs increased sharply from 2015. This map will eventually show the change in reported cases over a 10 year period, but for now shows the cases of chlamydia reported in 2016.

    The height of each county represents the number of cases reported, while the color depth represents the percentage of each county's population diagnosed with chlamydia.

    Click on any county for more detailed information, or choose a new region from the radio buttons in the lower right. It may be a bit slow to load so please be patient. I will probably have to switch to a 2D map in the future, but for now I like how this works even though it is slow.

    Data provided by the CDC and AtlasPlus, September 2018

    Denver Bridge Integrity

    This map shows the structural integrity of each bridge in Denver as rated by the FHWA in 2017.

    Bridges are determined to be structurally deficient if the Deck, Substructure, Superstructure, or Culvert receive a rating of 4 or below.

    Data provided by the FHWA and USGS, September 2018
