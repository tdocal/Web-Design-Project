    Denver Bridge Integrity

    Data provided by FHWA was joined with bridge locations provided by the city and county of Denver by structure ID. Only bridges within Denver county are shown. Bridge sufficency rating is determined by the deck condition, superstructure, substructure, and culvert condition. A rating of 7 or greater is considered good or better, 5-6 is considered fair, and 4 or below is poor.
